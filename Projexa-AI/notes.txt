06-03-2026
detection_model2.py detects following types of stuttering:
0 : NoStutteredWords
1 : WordRep
2 : SoundRep 
3 : Prolongation
4 : Interjection
5 : Block
It can also analyze long audio and detect stutter segments over 3s time instead of giving only one prediction for the whole file.

16-03-2026
The detection_model2.py code was enhanced to store chunk predictions and merge consecutive chunks with the same label into stutter segments, giving a more accurate representation of continuous stuttering events.
A weighted scoring system was introduced where different stutter types are assigned severity weights and combined with segment duration to compute an overall stuttering intensity score.
The final intensity score is then normalized and mapped to a severity level (Fluent, Mild, Moderate, Severe) for easier interpretation.

The upcoming work on the project will be based on the following research paper on stuttering:
https://arxiv.org/pdf/2501.15877
